Biryani

Ingredients: Rice, chicken, spices, yogurt, onions.

Procedure: Cook rice and chicken separately. Mix with spices and layer. Cook on low heat.

Bread Omelette

Ingredients: Bread, eggs, onions, tomatoes, spices.

Procedure: Beat eggs with spices. Dip bread in egg mixture and fry until golden.

Maggi

Ingredients: Maggi noodles, water, tastemaker.

Procedure: Boil water, add noodles and tastemaker. Cook for 2 minutes.

Carrot Halwa

Ingredients: Carrots, milk, sugar, ghee, nuts.

Procedure: Grate carrots and cook in milk until thick. Add sugar and ghee.
